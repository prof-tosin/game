How to play Karachi

  • Match the top discard by suit or rank.
  • Ace — wild, play anytime and choose the next suit.
  • 2 — next player draws 2 (stacks if answered with another 2).
  • 8 — skip the opponent's turn.
  • Can't play? Draw a card. If it's playable, you may play it right away.
  • First to empty their hand wins.
